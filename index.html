Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Ranked #1 in India

#41 Globally in THE Impact Rankings 2025

Dear All,

We are delighted to share a momentous achievement with our Amrita community!

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham ranked 1st in India and 41st in the world by the Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings 2025.

The Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings are the only global indicators to assess universities' progress towards the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The rankings include 2,152 universities from 125 countries and regions.

It's an acknowledgement of Amrita's commitment to remain an exemplary institution that creates Global Impact on society through Compassion-Driven Research and Education for Life. It's an education that harmonizes both scientific knowledge and spiritual learning to create a sustainable tomorrow for all.

We thank our students, faculty, staff, alumni, and partners who are influencing change, both nationally and globally, as we build for the future.
